Lesson Overview

  • History

    The history of the earth - divided into 100 lessons of 5 minutes each. Obviously, that's a lot to cover. So you'll get highlights, with bullet points you can actually remember. There are links to read more if you'd like to dig deeper. You'll get 2 days each week of history lessons, with a weekly review.

  • GEOGRAPHY

    For the most part, this includes a summary of every country of the world. With about 200 countries to be covered in about 100 lessons, there will be many lessons that cover more than a single sovereign state. The goal of these lessons is be able to identify each country on the map, and retain a few main ideas about each. As with the history lessons, you'll receive these geography lessons twice each week. View a recent email here.

  • MATH

    The goal of the math lessons will be to re-introduce readers to concepts they have been taught once in high school, including basic algebra, geometry, statistics, and calculus. You'll receive one math lesson each week, for a total of about 50 by the end of the year.

  • SCIENCE

    The science lessons will cover the basics of earth science, biology, chemistry, physics, and technology. You'll receive one science lesson each week, for a total of about 50 by the end of the year. View a recent science email here.

  • ARTS

    The arts is a general term that will cover world religions, art history, literature, and a bit of the classics. You'll receive one art lesson each week, for a total of about 50 by the end of the year.
