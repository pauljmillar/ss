It's Simple

Follow us on twitter. We'll tweet a few times every day on a different topic.

2. Get smarter

Read the tweets on your way to work, in the car, waiting for the bus. When you have 2 minutes, fit it in.

Lesson Overview

  • History

    The history of the earth - divided into 100 lessons of 5 minutes each. Obviously, that's a lot to cover. So you'll get highlights, with bullet points you can actually remember. There are links to read more if you'd like to dig deeper. You'll get one history lesson each week, in addition to a current events lesson.

  • GEOGRAPHY

    For the most part, this includes a summary of every country of the world. Covering about 200 countries, one per week, is going to take some time. Stick with it and just keep reading your email, and the world will begin to feel a lot smaller than it used to.

  • MATH

    The math lessons will channel the energy of your high school math teacher(s), covering basic algebra, geometry, statistics, and calculus. You'll receive one math lesson each week, for a total of about 50 by the end of the year.

  • SCIENCE

    The science lessons will cover the basics of earth science, biology, chemistry, physics, and technology. You'll receive one science lesson each week, for a total of about 50 by the end of the year.

  • ARTS

    The arts is a general term that will cover world religions, art history, literature, and a bit of the classics. You'll receive one art lesson each week, for a total of about 50 by the end of the year.
