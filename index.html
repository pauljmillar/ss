Stuff you should have learned in high school

We were exposed to a lot in high school. If you're like us, you don't remember much. Our solution to this problem, like all problems, is to check twitter more.

Throughout the day we're tweeting highlights to lessons on math, science, geography, history, and the arts. While you have a minute during the day, read our highlights. If you want to dig deeper, follow the links back to Wikipedia. It's that simple.

History

The lessons start with the history of earth and move into the great civilizations of the world.

Science

Sciene lessons cover earth science, biology, chemistry, physics, and technology.

Math

Math via twitter can get pretty heavy, click through to dig deeper: algebra, geometry, statistics, calculus.

The Arts

Lessons will cover literature, theater, art history, the classics, and world religions.

Geography

Each lesson covers highlights of one of the 196 countries.
